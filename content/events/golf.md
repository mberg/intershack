## 25th Fall Golf Classic (2019)

Our 25th Annual Fall golf tournament was held as part of our 50th anniversary weekend. Almost 100 participants turned out on a beautiful day for some good competition.

### 2019 Winners
- **Low Gross**: Men - Gary Griffiths, Women - Lorraine Leduc
- **Low Net - Intershacker**: Men - Guy Quesnel, Women - Lynda Fraser  
- **Longest Drive**: Men - Tomas Syrovotka, Women - Louise Aiken
- **Closest to the Pin**: Men - John Cheffins, Women - Paddi Griffiths
- **Best Putter**: Men - Palu Toppi, Women - Christine Tully

Special congratulations to Leslie Syrovotka and Kate Bradley who won 1/2 season ski passes for 2019-2020!
