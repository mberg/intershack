## 2025 Events Calendar

### Saturday, November 29, 2025 - Intershack Welcome Cocktail
**Location:** Tower Bar
**Time:** 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

The Intershack Welcome Cocktail will be held at the Tower Bar on Saturday November 29, from 4:00-6:00.

It is an opportunity to connect with old friends and meet new friends and hear about upcoming Intershack events.

Please let us know by Friday November 14th if you will be joining us so we can plan the food accordingly.

We look forward to seeing you at this event and future events throughout the season and here's hoping to a repeat of the 2024/2025 winter snow!

### Sunday, August 31, 2025 - Annual Labor Day Golf Tournament
**Location:** Jay Peak Golf Course  
Join us for our biggest event of the year! This popular tournament brings together members from Montreal and Vermont for a day of golf, prizes, and celebration.

### Sunday, April 26, 2025 - Jay Peak Tail Gate Party
**Location:** Jay Peak Resort  
End-of-season celebration at Jay Peak with food, drinks, and mountain memories.

### Saturday, March 15, 2025 - Saint Patrick's Day Cocktail Party
Join us for our traditional St. Patrick's Day celebration with drinks, food, and Irish spirit!

### Saturday, February 8, 2025 - Valentine's Day Dinner
A romantic evening for couples to celebrate Valentine's Day together as part of the Intershack community.
